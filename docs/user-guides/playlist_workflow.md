# Playlist Playback Flow

## 1. Selecting a Playlist
Users can enter a playlist from:
- The **Home page**
- The **Playlist page**

## 2. Inside the Playlist
Once inside the playlist:
- User can press **Play All**, or
- Select any video from the list to start playback in playlist mode.

## 3. Video Playback
- The selected video begins playing automatically.
- Playlist mode is activated.

## 4. Transition to the Next Video
At the end of a video:
- A **transition card** appears showing the metadata of the next video.
- Playback automatically proceeds to the next video.

## 5. Fullscreen Mode Behavior
If the video is being watched in fullscreen:
- During routing to the next video, a **fullscreen prompt** appears asking the user to continue watching in fullscreen.
- The user may tap the screen or press **Enter** to keep playing in fullscreen mode.

## 6. Completing the Playlist
When the final video in the playlist finishes:
- The user is automatically routed back to the **playlist page** of the selected playlist.
